Product Supply Chain Application (Blockchain)

This is a blockchain-based application that utilises MetaMask wallet to store information

It aims to provide proof of provenance of each product in the chain for transparency between customers and suppliers

It aims to notify customers or suppliers if the product they are servicing/handling is contaminated

Suppliers will:

Be able to add products information and activity in the chain

Be notified if the product they are servicing is contaminated

Paused and unable to create a transaction of the product is contaminated

Be able to see the list of all contaminated products for tracking and product recalls

Be able to tag a product as contaminated and conduct actions if necessary

Customers will:

Be able to search for a product and see its origin

Be notified if a product they are searching for is contaminated

Be able to see the list of all contaminated products

Programming Languages Used:

HTML & CSS (Front-End)

Javascript (Back-End)

MetaMask Wallet (Storage)
